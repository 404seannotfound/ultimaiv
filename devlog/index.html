A Chronicle of the Casting

The First Stroke

9ea36ae · 23 may 2026

On the first evening the wagon was wheeled to the edge of the fair. Eight blank cards were sketched in silverpoint upon the parchment, the Gypsy's voice was rendered in sixteen lines, twenty-eight parables were written down, and the prototype was set down whole — a single page that opened to the candlelight.

The Eight Cards Spread

56e92f3 · 23 may 2026

Once the wagon stood, the cards needed to be touched. A small chamber was carved within for the seeker who wished only to browse the deck — a /cards door, accessible without entering the ceremony. Twenty-eight parable scenes were given to the Gypsy's voice, so that each dilemma might be spoken aloud as it appeared. The deck-browser opened from a small pill at the end of every reading.

The Crooked Path Made Straight

ddc95ab · 23 may 2026

The /cards door, on first opening, led nowhere — a routing tangle between the page and the folder of card-images, which shared the same name. A signpost was placed: shim an index inside the cards directory, simplify the _redirects, and the door swung wide. The wandering pilgrim arrived where they meant to.

The Wall Removed

bc3389d · 23 may 2026

What had been two screens at the end of a reading — a permalink panel and a separate deck-browser — were joined into one. The chosen virtue's card now opens in the deck-browser itself, with its companions arrayed beneath, the permalink lettered in the footer, and a new section painted at the bottom of every card: four short suggestions for cultivating that virtue, in the same archaic register as the Gypsy's voice.

The Stuck Gear

3ad855f · 23 may 2026

Two stones lay across the road. First: the deck-browser at /cards/ rendered every word but no images, because its assets resolved against the wrong path — all URLs were rewritten absolute. Second: the casting did not auto-advance from its final reveal, because the reveal layer covered the dialogue panel and hid the only button. The gear was untangled; the ceremony walks itself to the end now.

The Margin Measured

301543b · 23 may 2026

Upon final viewing, the thumbnail strip sat atop the cultivation list, hiding the last two bullets. The bottom margin of the deck-browser was widened to give the strip its own air. A small skip-ahead link was added beneath the Enter the Wagon button on the intro screen, for those who came only for the deck.

The Chronicle Sealed

04d4f02 · 23 may 2026

Finally the README was brought current — both ways in, the deck-browser, the cultivation suggestions, the new audio paths, the deploy shim. A fan-tribute license note was added at the bottom, naming what is mine and what belongs to the older makers. The signboard was hung, the wax seal pressed.

Seven commits, one evening, one ceremony.
